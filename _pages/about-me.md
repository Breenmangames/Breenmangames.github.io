Who am i?

Born in the back end of nowhere in County Tyrone, Northern Ireland, in the dark ages 1995, I started my work journey like alot of people in my area, which was customer service based Jobs.
After a decade of customer facing roles and team leadership roles within that industry i decided that it was time to make an extreme change in my career trajectory and look at what was missing in my life.

After some deep contemplation, i realised that since i was a young adult i would always ponder what games would i love to consume but wasn't currently in the market, whether this would be a board game, card game or video game.
I would have conceptual designs written down and in the cases of some of the card games i would have a small deck of cards ready and play tested with some friends during a party or after playing in our local card shop!

That realisation has led me down this new path of game design! I am currently a Year 1 student in Ulster University and aim to make a long and fruitful career in this space!
